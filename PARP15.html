Protein mono-ADP-ribosyltransferase PARP15

Enables NAD+ binding activity; pentosyltransferase activity; and transcription corepressor activity. Involved in negative regulation of transcription by RNA polymerase II; protein mono-ADP-ribosylation; and protein poly-ADP-ribosylation. Predicted to be active in cytoplasm and nucleus.
