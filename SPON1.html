Spondin-1

Predicted to be an extracellular matrix structural constituent. Predicted to be involved in cell adhesion. Predicted to act upstream of or within positive regulation of protein binding activity; positive regulation of protein processing; and regulation of amyloid precursor protein catabolic process. Located in extracellular space. Colocalizes with collagen-containing extracellular matrix.
