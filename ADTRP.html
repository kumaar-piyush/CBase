Androgen-dependent TFPI-regulating protein

Enables hydrolase activity. Involved in several processes, including cell migration involved in sprouting angiogenesis; negative regulation of secretion by cell; and positive regulation of macromolecule metabolic process. Located in caveola and cell surface.
